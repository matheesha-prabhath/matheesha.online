I'm Matheesha Prabhath — a fresh graduate from SLIIT with a BSc (Hons) in Information Technology specializing in Software Engineering. Born and raised in Passara, Sri Lanka.

I got into software because I wanted to build things that actually work and matter. Over time, I picked up creative skills in video editing and design, which turned out to be just as valuable as the code — clients always need someone who can do both.

Outside the screen, I was a Company Sergeant Major in the National Cadet Corps and still carry that discipline into everything I do. I also played chess competitively and spent a lot of time behind a camera as a photographer for SLIIT's Faculty of Computing Media Unit.
